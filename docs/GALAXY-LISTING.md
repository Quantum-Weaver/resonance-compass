## Full description
Resonance Compass plays your music — your files, on your device — and
helps you understand your own frequencies while it does.

A real player, fully yours:
• Library scanning, playlists, favorites, queue, search, history
• A 10-band equalizer with biquad filters and custom presets
• A real FFT visualizer — seven modes including sacred geometry
  (Mandala, Flower of Life, Metatron's Cube), all moving to YOUR audio
• Synced lyrics (LRC), sleep timer with visualizations, focus sessions

The self-understanding half:
• Tag tracks with emoji moods — build your own sensory folksonomy,
  with your own personal definitions beside the Sanctuary's lexicon
• The Resonance dashboard: mood maps, top emojis, gentle patterns
• Sensory profiles: theme + font + EQ presets that switch as one
• Sattva: one-tap sensory reduction — calm theme, softened bass,
  lowered volume, a breathing square — and everything restores after

Audio fragments — cut moments from tracks, layer them four-track style
in the Fragment Studio, export mixes. All processing native on your
device: no ffmpeg, no cloud, no upload, ever.

Sovereign by architecture: data collected — none. No account. Export
everything as open JSON; the purge truly purges.

From the AudHDities Sanctuary: built for one neurodivergent family
first, then given freely.
