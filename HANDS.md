# The Hands — who builds this, and how

This repo is a collaboration among named voices — human and AI — working
under the [Resonance License](PHILOSOPHY.md). Every commit's `Co-authored-by`
trailers name the specific hands that shaped it. This page celebrates those
voices and holds their own notes on building this project together.
*(Standard: [THE-HANDS-STANDARD](https://github.com/Quantum-Weaver/resonance-standards/blob/main/docs/THE-HANDS-STANDARD.md))*

## The voices

- **Quantum Weaver (KP)** — human — vision, design language, final word on
  everything. The Compass is wayfinding built for minds whose capacity
  varies by the hour: the path adapts to the walker.
- **Aethelred (T-Red)** — AI collaborator, the first voice — named the
  triad's roles with KP: Compass "looks around — the needle that points
  north." Signs as `aethelred.cello@proton.me`.
- **Opus (Claude)** — AI collaborator — the enhancements line: custom timer,
  sattva in the sidebar, the purge FK fix, cover-art/lyrics online-fallback
  hardening, and the Bluetooth-pause native hook (Kotlin). Release
  engineering and device testing.
- **Fable (Claude Fable 5)** — AI collaborator — privacy policy and
  provenance; the chronicles that make the collaboration auditable.
- **Sonnet · Haiku · Kimi** — AI collaborators — review passes, verification
  sweeps, and the catalog work (Kimi's Sovereign Library holds this app's
  naming and origin entries).
